Flower Types

Explore the different types of flowers available in our collection.

Rose

Roses are classic and romantic flowers known for their beauty and fragrance.

Daisy

Daisies are simple and cheerful flowers often associated with childhood and innocence.

Jasmine

Jasmine is a highly fragrant flower often used in perfumes and traditional ceremonies.

Lavender

Lavender is known for its soothing fragrance and beautiful purple flowers.

Lily

Lilies are large, elegant flowers often associated with purity and spiritual meanings.

Orchid

Orchids are exotic and delicate flowers admired for their unique shapes and long-lasting blooms.

Sunflower

Sunflowers are tall, bright flowers that turn toward the sun, symbolizing joy and energy.

Tulip

Tulips are graceful spring-blooming flowers known for their cup-shaped petals and vibrant colors.

Peony

Peonies are lush, full-petaled flowers admired for their soft texture and romantic appearance.

Carnation

Carnations are ruffled flowers known for their long-lasting blooms and wide range of colors.

Chrysanthemum

Chrysanthemums are vibrant flowers that bloom in autumn and symbolize joy and positivity.

Gardenia

Gardenias are creamy white flowers famous for their intense fragrance and elegant look.

Anemone

Anemones are delicate flowers with dark centers and vibrant petals.

Magnolia

Magnolias are elegant tree flowers known for their large petals and pleasant fragrance.
